HOW WE WORK

Elevate helps Myanmar young people to get the right mentors for their education, life and career enhancement. We have one on one, group/team, e-mentoring (Zoom/Skype) and peer mentoring. Elevate carefully select a mentor to partner with and only work with those who are self made people in their respective fields.
